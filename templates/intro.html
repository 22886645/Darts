The sport of darts should be considered as one of the branches of sport and even military training, which is considered as an art: the art of throwing darts.
The aim of this website is to introduce you to the game of dart

Although not much is known about how darts were invented, almost everyone agrees that the game has been around for a long time. The game has changed a lot over the years, from a mere scoring game to a game that requires not only a high level of focus, but also strong math to succeed. Currently, this game is played in different models and the rules of darts are different depending on the type.
